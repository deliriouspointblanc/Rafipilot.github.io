My Cadet Story

Back in September of 2021 my dad forced me to go to the Air Cadets open evening, where I was facinated and started my journey as a Air Cadet later that September. So far I have enjoyed every momement of it. We get to do archery, shooting, flying and much more. I highly recommend Air Cadets to anyone between the ages of 12 and 20.
