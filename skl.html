My homeschooling journey

Up to year 5(10 years old), I went to Eaton house the manor in Clapham. in year 6 I left Eaton House and became home-schooled along with my younger brother who was in year 3 at the time and at the start of 2019 we started having tutors come to our house regularly. Within two years we had both completed foundation maths with a grade 5 and the year after that i had done higher maths and physics passing both with 8s(a*s). Soon after this i completed further maths with a 7 and now am studying for another 4 GCSEs and 2 A levels (maths and physics). Along with academic stuff, we had a lot of extra curricula such as taekwondo, cricket, tennis and I am now a air cadet at 33f battersea squadron.
