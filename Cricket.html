Cricket

From the age of 4 to 9 I attended cricket training at Lords indoor Training Centre where i found my love for the game. At 9 years old i joined Roehampton cricket club have played over 100 matches over the 4 years I have been at the club. I think that cricket is a great game and that it is important for all children to have a sport that they love. I have also participated in borough level cricket at under 10's and under 12's at Middlesex inner London borough.
